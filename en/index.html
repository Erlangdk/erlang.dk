The Holiday Home

A cosy kalmar holiday cottage and guest annex. The holiday cottage has 3 bedrooms, each with 2 beds (4 of the beds consist of 2 bunk beds), a bathroom and a combined dining- and living room with a woodburning stove (+ electric heating). The guest annex has two beds. That makes 8 beds altogether.

The Garden

A beautiful garden at 2.613 m2 that is child-friendly. In the garden you will among other things find 4 terraces and a fire ring. Here is part of the garden, and behind that you see the hill "Basbjerg".

Beautiful Surroundings

The holiday cottage is located in "Mols Bjerge" which means that the vicinity is beautiful, and there is a neat bathing beach approximately 500 metres from the holiday home.
