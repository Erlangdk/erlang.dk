Your first dinner

We know how lovely it can be if the first meal is ready and well prepared, when you initiate your holiday.

That is why we have decided that you can – if you want to – order a homemade meal.

We cook a chosen course and make it ready for you. We place the dish in the fridge so you can heat it in the stove yourselves

If you want to we can provide something to drink as well (soft drinks, juice, beer, wine). And there is drink water straight from the tap.

Furthermore, with each course a green salad is included.
